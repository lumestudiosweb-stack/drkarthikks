About Dr. Karthik

A Doctor Who Listens,
Explains, & Guides

Dr. Karthik K S is a Surgical Oncologist based in Mangaluru with a superspeciality MCh degree in Surgical Oncology. His practice is centred on the belief that every cancer patient deserves not only technically precise surgery but also clear answers, emotional support, and a treatment plan they fully understand.

From the very first consultation, Dr. Karthik takes the time to explain diagnoses in simple language, outline all available options, and walk patients and their families through what to expect — before, during, and after surgery. His calm confidence and genuine warmth have earned him a 4.9-star rating across hundreds of verified reviews.

He specialises in oral cancer, thyroid surgery, breast surgery, and gynaecological oncology. Whether you are navigating a new diagnosis or seeking a second opinion, Dr. Karthik provides the clarity and conviction that transforms anxiety into action.
